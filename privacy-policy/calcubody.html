Privacy Policy

Effective Date: October 16, 2025

Last Updated: October 16, 2025

CalcuBody ("we", "our", "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use our mobile fitness application.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

When you use CalcuBody, you may voluntarily provide the following information:

  • Personal Health Data: Weight, height, age, gender (for calculator features)
  • Fitness Data: Workout preferences, exercise history, saved routines
  • Profile Information: Name, email address (if you choose to create an account)
  • User Preferences: Theme settings, notification preferences, favorite workouts

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, app version
  • Usage Data: Features used, screens viewed, time spent in app
  • Performance Data: App crashes, errors, loading times (for bug fixes)

1.3 Information We Do NOT Collect

  • Payment or financial information (app is free)
  • Precise GPS location
  • Photos or camera access (except for exercise GIF demonstrations)
  • Contacts or call logs
  • Social media account credentials

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information for the following purposes:

  • Provide App Features: BMI, calorie, body fat, protein, water intake, and ideal weight calculations
  • Personalization: Save your workout preferences, theme choices, and favorite exercises
  • App Improvement: Analyze usage patterns to enhance user experience and add new features
  • Performance Optimization: Monitor crashes and errors to improve app stability
  • Communication: Send important updates or notifications (only if you opt-in)

3. Data Storage and Security

3.1 Local Storage

Most of your data is stored locally on your device using secure storage methods. This includes:

  • Your health metrics (weight, height, BMI results)
  • Saved workouts and exercise history
  • App preferences and settings

3.2 Security Measures

  • Data encryption for sensitive information
  • Secure data transmission protocols
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • No third-party access to your health data without consent

Note: While we implement industry-standard security measures, no method of electronic storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security but strive to protect your data to the best of our ability.

4. Data Sharing and Third-Party Services

4.1 We Do NOT Sell Your Data

CalcuBody does not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

4.2 Third-Party Services We May Use

  • Analytics Tools: To understand app usage and improve features (e.g., Google Analytics, Firebase)
  • Crash Reporting: To identify and fix bugs (e.g., Sentry, Crashlytics)
  • Cloud Storage (Optional): If you choose to backup data to cloud services

These services have their own privacy policies and only receive anonymized or minimal data necessary for their function.

5. Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Access: View and export your saved data at any time within the app
  • Deletion: Delete your account and all associated data from app settings
  • Correction: Update or modify your profile information and health metrics
  • Opt-Out: Disable analytics or notifications in app settings
  • Data Portability: Export your workout history and health data

6. Children's Privacy

CalcuBody is not intended for users under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately, and we will delete such information.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. We will notify you of significant changes through:

  • In-app notifications
  • Email (if you provided one)
  • Updated "Last Modified" date at the top of this policy

Your continued use of CalcuBody after changes indicates acceptance of the updated policy.

8. International Users

CalcuBody is available globally. If you use the app outside of India, please note that your data may be transferred to and processed in India or other countries where our servers are located. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for international data transfers.

9. California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA):

  • Right to know what personal information is collected
  • Right to delete your personal information
  • Right to opt-out of data sale (we don't sell data)
  • Right to non-discrimination for exercising your rights

10. GDPR Compliance (European Users)

If you are in the European Economic Area (EEA), you have rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR):

  • Right of access to your personal data
  • Right to rectification of inaccurate data
  • Right to erasure ("right to be forgotten")
  • Right to data portability
  • Right to object to processing

11. Your Consent

By downloading, installing, and using CalcuBody, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this Privacy Policy and consent to the collection, use, and storage of your information as described herein.
